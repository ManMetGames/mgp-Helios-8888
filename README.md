# MGP_2526
 The assessment repo for 2526

Mechanics Implemented
- Dash (Primary mechanic)
- Grappling Hook (Secondary Mechanic)

Control Scheme
- WASD To move
- Space bar to Jump/Double Jump
- Left Shift to Dash towards the ground
- Right Click to Grapple a point in front of you
- Hold Right Click to be dragged towards the grapple point
- Release Right Click to end your grapple early

Ability Information
- Dash has a cooldown of 5 seconds
- Grappling has a cooldown of 4 seconds
